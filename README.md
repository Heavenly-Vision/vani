# Privacy Policy — Vani App
Last updated: 18th June 2026

**1. Who we are**
Vani ("we", "our", "us") is an AI-powered social media caption writing app. We operate at [your website URL]. Contact us at [your email address].

**2. What data we collect and why**
Account information: When you sign in with Google or Facebook, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo. We use this only to create and identify your Vani account.
Photos: When you take or select a photo to generate captions, the photo is sent to our AI service (Anthropic Claude) for analysis. Photos used for Instagram posting are temporarily stored in Firebase Storage to obtain a public URL required by Instagram's API. Photos are not used for any other purpose and are not shared with third parties beyond what is required to operate the app.

Social media credentials: We store your Facebook Page access token and Instagram account ID securely on your device using Android's encrypted storage (EncryptedSharedPreferences). These credentials are never transmitted to our servers. They are used only to post content to your connected accounts when you choose to post.

Usage data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data (screens visited, features used). This helps us improve the app. No personally identifiable information is included in analytics data.
Post history: Your posting history is stored locally on your device using Room database. This data does not leave your device except as described above.

**3. AI processing of your photos**
Vani uses Anthropic's Claude API to analyse your photos and generate captions. Your photo is transmitted to Anthropic's servers for this purpose. Anthropic's Privacy Policy governs how they handle data sent to their API. We do not store photos on our servers after caption generation is complete. Please review Anthropic's privacy policy at https://www.anthropic.com/privacy.

**4. Data sharing**
We share your data with: (a) Anthropic (photo analysis), (b) Firebase/Google (authentication, storage, analytics), (c) Facebook/Meta (when you post via the app). We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers.

**5. Data retention and deletion**
You can delete your Vani account at any time from the Settings screen. Deleting your account removes your Firebase Auth user record and clears all locally stored data including tokens, post history, and preferences.

**6. Children**
Vani is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Users under 13 must not use this app.

**7. Your rights (GDPR, CCPA)**
You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. Contact us at [your email] to exercise these rights. For users in the EU, we comply with GDPR. For California residents, we comply with CCPA.

**8. Changes to this policy**
We will notify users of material changes to this policy through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
